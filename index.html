Market Strategies

How We Evaluate
Every Position

01
52-WEEK BREAKOUT STRATEGY
Stocks making new 52-week highs with above-average volume confirm institutional buying. We look for stocks that break above the 52W high on 2x+ average volume with RSI between 55–70 — not overbought, but with momentum. Sector tailwind confirmation is mandatory. These are momentum-continuation buys with a 15–20% upside target.
↗ MOMENTUM PLAY · SHORT–MID TERM
02
52-WEEK LOW ACCUMULATION
Stocks within 10% of 52W lows with strong fundamentals (Debt/Equity <1, ROE >15%) represent deep-value opportunities. We require a bullish reversal candle (hammer, engulfing) on daily/weekly charts before entry. Position sizing is conservative — 25–30% of full allocation initially, averaging down only on volume confirmation.
↘ CONTRARIAN VALUE · LONG TERM SIP
03
CRYPTO DOLLAR-COST AVERAGING
For crypto, DCA (monthly SIP on WazirX/CoinDCX) beats timing in volatile markets. We recommend 70% BTC + ETH core allocation, 20% in high-conviction altcoins (SOL, MATIC), 10% speculative. Never invest more than 5–10% of total portfolio in crypto. Use a hardware wallet for holdings above ₹5 lakh.
₿ PORTFOLIO ALLOCATION · ALL TERMS
04
NIFTY 50 INDEX + SATELLITE
Core-satellite approach: 60% Nifty50 index fund (UTI/Nippon), 20% sector-specific (IT, Banking, Defence ETFs), 20% direct equity in high-conviction stocks. This ensures market-matching returns on core while alpha generation via satellites. Rebalance quarterly. This is India's most time-tested wealth creation framework.
📊 DIVERSIFICATION · LONG TERM
05
STOP-LOSS & RISK MANAGEMENT
Non-negotiable rule: 7–8% stop-loss on all direct equity trades. For investments, use 15% trailing stop. Risk 1–2% of total capital per trade. Never average down on a falling stock without fundamental trigger. Set SL at the time of entry, not after loss — emotional decisions destroy portfolios.
🛡 CAPITAL PROTECTION · ALL TRADES
06
SECTOR ROTATION WATCH
Money rotates across sectors every 6–18 months. Current rotation: Capital Goods → IT Services → Banking. Watch for budget cycle plays (Infra, Defence) and global tailwinds (IT exports on USD strength). This week's top sector: Green Energy & EV infrastructure benefiting from govt PLI schemes and RBI rate cut expectations.
